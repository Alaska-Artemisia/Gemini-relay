I started Me + Lia because I kept ordering dresses that disappointed me. Beautiful in the photos. Wrong in my hands. The cotton too thin. The colour not quite right. The return process a small fight I didn't want to have.

Six times in one year. I stopped ordering. So I started making instead.

Me + Lia is built around one simple idea: a dress should earn its place in your wardrobe. Not by being precious — by being worn. To the market, to the meeting, to the wedding outside the city, to the slow Tuesday at home when you just want to feel like yourself.

We design slowly. Small batches. 100% premium cotton.

We offer 30 days on your first dress because a dress should earn its place. If it doesn't — in real life, in real light, worn to real occasions you've been looking forward to — it shouldn't cost you anything to find that out. Free return shipping. Full refund. No argument.

That's the promise. That's why this brand exists.
