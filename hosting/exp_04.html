Every piece begins as a length of cloth on the worktable.

We keep our prints small on purpose: the kind you notice up close, a scatter of blossoms, a fine run of stitching, not from across the room. It's a quieter way to dress, and a more lasting one.

Our cotton is chosen to be lived in: soft enough to wear all day, sturdy enough to wear for years. Crafted from 100% premium cotton, cut slowly, and made to be worn often.
